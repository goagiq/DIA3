Chinese Strategic Philosophy

Core Principle: "兵者，詭道也" (War is the way of deception)

Key Characteristics: Indirect approach, harmony and balance, long-term perspective, adaptability, information asymmetry

Modern Application: South China Sea strategy through economic integration and cultural influence

Russian Strategic Philosophy

Core Principle: "Сила есть право" (Might makes right)

Key Characteristics: Direct approach, great power mentality, historical victimhood, zero-sum thinking, authoritarian efficiency

Modern Application: Ukraine conflict through military action and energy weaponization
