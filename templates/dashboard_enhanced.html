Strategic Summary

Deterrence Enhancement
Would provide Pakistan with a credible conventional deterrent against superior naval forces, creating genuine strategic uncertainty for adversaries and enhanced escalation control mechanisms.
Economic Viability
Massive economic costs likely beyond Pakistan's current capacity, with program costs representing 6-11% of GDP and 25-50% of defense budget, raising significant sustainability concerns.
Regional Impact
Fundamental shift in regional naval balance with high risk of arms race escalation, significant Indian responses, and international reactions from major powers.
Strategic Recommendation
Adopt realistic phased implementation approach focusing on quality over quantity, with 10-15 submarines over 10 years for sustainable strategic outcomes.
