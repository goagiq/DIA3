Cambodia J-10 Fighter Jet Acquisition

Comprehensive Strategic Analysis of Geopolitical Implications
Analysis Date: August 24, 2025

Executive Summary

Military Modernization

Cambodia's acquisition of 50 J-10 fighter jets represents a significant military modernization, transforming the country from having no modern fighter aircraft to possessing a substantial air combat capability.

Strategic Alignment

The acquisition will strengthen Cambodia-China relations while straining US-Cambodia ties, creating a notable shift in regional diplomatic dynamics and strategic partnerships.

Regional Balance

Regional military balance will shift moderately toward China-aligned states, potentially triggering responses from other regional powers and affecting ASEAN unity.

Economic Dependencies

Economic dependencies on China will increase significantly, particularly in defense equipment, technical services, training, and spare parts supply chains.

Escalation Risks

Escalation risks are moderate, with potential for regional instability, arms race dynamics, and increased military posturing among neighboring states.

ASEAN Unity

ASEAN unity may be tested by diverging security interests, as member states respond differently to Cambodia's military modernization and China's growing influence.
